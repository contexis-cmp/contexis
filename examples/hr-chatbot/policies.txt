Paid time off (PTO): Full-time employees accrue 1.5 days per month.
Parental leave: Up to 12 weeks paid leave for primary caregiver; 6 weeks for secondary caregiver.
Remote work: Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week with manager approval.
Code of conduct: Treat colleagues with respect and report harassment to HR immediately.
Benefits: Health, dental, and vision coverage begin on the first day of employment.
